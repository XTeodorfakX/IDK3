Chorizo & mozzarella gnocchi bake

Ingredients

1 tbsp olive oil

1 onion, finely chopped

2 garlic cloves, crushed

120g chorizo, diced

2 x 400g cans chopped tomatoes

1 tsp caster sugar

600g fresh gnocchi

125g mozzarella ball, cut into chunks

small bunch of basil, torn

green salad, to serve

Method

STEP 1 Heat the oil in a medium pan over a medium heat. Fry the onion and garlic for 8-10 mins until soft. Add the chorizo and fry for 5 mins more. Tip in the tomatoes and sugar, and season. Bring to a simmer, then add the gnocchi and cook for 8 mins, stirring often, until soft. Heat the grill to high.

STEP 2 Stir Â¾ of the mozzarella and most of the basil through the gnocchi. Divide the mixture between six ovenproof ramekins, or put in one baking dish. Top with the remaining mozzarella, then grill for 3 mins, or until the cheese is melted and golden. Season, scatter over the remaining basil and serve with green salad.

Bacon and mushroom breakfast hash recipe

Ingredients

4 tsp oil

250g leftover bread (ciabatta or baguette works well), cut into 3cm cubes

250g smoked bacon, cut into small pieces

300g chestnut mushrooms, cut into quarters

1 tsp garlic powder

1 tsp onion powder

250g cherry tomatoes, halved

4 eggs

75g mature Cheddar, grated

Method

STEP 1 Heat 3 tsp oil over a medium-high heat in a large, deep frying pan. Add the bread cubes and cook until starting to crisp or crisped all over (depending on how you like it).

STEP 2 Remove the bread cubes to a plate, put the pan back on the heat and add the remaining 1 tsp oil.

STEP 3 Add the bacon, mushrooms, garlic powder and onion powder; season. Fry for 5-10 mins until everything is cooked through and the water from the mushrooms has cooked off.

STEP 4 Put the bread cubes back in the pan along with the tomatoes and stir to mix through. Preheat the grill to high.

STEP 5 Make 4 spaces in the hash and break an egg into each one, sprinkle the cheese over and around and cook for 5 mins until the bottoms of the eggs are starting to cook. Pop under a hot grill to finish off and melt the cheese.
